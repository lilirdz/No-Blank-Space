Our Mission Statement

The NoBlankSpace Project is a multifaceted campaign that zeros in on the need for art as a tool for diplomacy and conflict resolution. Utilizing murals as a canvas for exposing and shedding light on the untold narratives lost in the power struggle of office politics in most large scale conflicts. NoBlankSpace aims to bring together people on opposing sides of a dispute to create a visual depiction of their experiences, integrating multiple vantage points. This symbolic but tangible gesture of camaraderie brings diplomacy outside of institutions and into the hands of the people. Through the creation of these mural spaces, communities will begin a healing process spurred on by their collaborative efforts. We are committed to promoting impassioned expression of personal narratives, ideas, and visions of the future.

Our Goal

What NoBlankSpace aims to do is to create beautiful, powerful murals that change the way people think about different issues; issues such as gentrification, oppression, and conflict on local and potentially global scales. We plan to use blank spaces -- otherwise unused, or empty wall areas that can be transformed -- in order to make meaningful spaces where artists and activists alike can come together.
